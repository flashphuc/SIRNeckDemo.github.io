Design a Shirt

English Spread Collar

Design a Shirt
Details

One of our most popular business collars which features a conservative spread width and versatile dimensions. The English Spread collar frames different face shapes really well and looks incredible both open collar and with a tie. The spread width is perfect for accommodating both large and small tie knots with ease, and the collar band height and collar point length are flattering on a variety of different builds. The English Spread was updated in 2016 to include a premium, stiffer collar band which gives the collar a cleaner appearance and helps it stand up better when worn under a jacket.

Specs

2 3/4" collar points, 5" spread, 1" front collar band height, 1 3/8" rear collar band height, 3/8" tie space, stiff fused interlining, removable collar stays.
